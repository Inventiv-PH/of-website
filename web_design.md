**Website Brief | OF Promo Automation Tool** 

**Goal**  
Build a high-conversion, professional, SaaS-style landing \+ signup experience for an OnlyFans promo automation platform.

*The site should:*

* Feel like real software (Stripe-like credibility)  
* Be short, direct, and clear  
* Minimize friction to download \+ purchase  
* Avoid hype language  
* Communicate: automated subscriber accounts \+ engagement automation, with emphasis on quality and realistic behavior

**Primary Audience**

* OnlyFans agency owners/managers  
* Advanced solo creators  
* Promo operators active in Telegram \+ IG/Twitter/Reddit promo ecosystems  
  (Assume younger, 18–35, operator-minded; responds best to clarity over corporate fluff.)

**Brand / Visual Direction**  
*Theme*

* Default: Dark mode  
* Also support light mode (toggle)  
* Look/feel: Technical SaaS dashboard, minimal, clean, premium

*Color Theme*

* Background (primary): \#0F1115  
* Background (secondary): \#1A1D23  
* Text primary: \#E6E6E6  
* Text secondary: \#9AA0A6  
* Accent (primary brand accent): \#8B2E3C (muted crimson)  
* Borders/dividers: subtle greys (low contrast)

*Notes*

* Avoid strong blues (do not mimic OnlyFans branding)  
* Use accent sparingly (buttons, highlights, small emphasis)

*Typography*

* Headings: Manrope  
* Body/UI: Inter  
* Use modern spacing, strong hierarchy, generous whitespace

*Imagery / Graphics*  
Use software-style visuals, not stock photos.

* UI screenshots or realistic UI mockups (blur sensitive areas if needed)  
* Minimal diagrams: simple step flow icons  
* Avoid: cartoon bots, Telegram screenshots, “bot farm” imagery, fake profiles

*Website Constraints*

* Keep to few pages max (recommended 2–4 total)  
* Must be fast, simple, mobile responsive  
* Focus: landing → download → pay → use  
* No headaches. No complex funnels.

*Copy / Tone Rules*

* Direct, operator language  
* Short sentences  
* No emojis  
* Avoid legal-risk language and absolute claims:  
  * Do NOT use: “undetectable,” “guaranteed,” “safe from bans”  
  * Avoid: “explode your growth,” “get rich,” overly hype phrasing  
* Be transparent about automation:  
  * Use “automated subscriber accounts,” “automated engagement”  
  * “Bots” can be used, but keep it technical and minimal

**Site Architecture**   
Page 1: Landing (Primary)  
Purpose: explain offer → build trust → drive download / pricing.
